About Me.

Chef

I'm France Michel, I was born and raised in Haiti, discovered my love for cooking alongside my parents.I honed my skills at Anne Marie Desvarieux and Florida International University. I am passionate about sharing Haitian cuisine. I run my own catering business, Epic Catering & Planning Services LLC and work as a private chef.
